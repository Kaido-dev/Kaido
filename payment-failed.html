Common Solutions

Card Issues

  • Check your card number is correct
  • Verify expiry date is in the future
  • Ensure CVV code is correct
  • Check if your card supports online payments

Insufficient Funds

  • Check your account balance
  • Verify your spending limit
  • Contact your bank if needed
  • Try a different payment method

Bank Security

  • Your bank might have blocked the transaction
  • Check for security alerts from your bank
  • Contact your bank to approve the payment
  • Try again after bank confirmation

Technical Issues

  • Clear your browser cache
  • Try a different browser
  • Check your internet connection
  • Disable browser extensions temporarily
